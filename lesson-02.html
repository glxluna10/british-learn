🎯 Learning Objectives

Setelah menyelesaikan lesson ini, kamu akan bisa:

  • Memahami fungsi Present Continuous dan bedanya dengan Present Simple.
  • Menggunakan am/is/are + V-ing dengan benar dalam kalimat positif, negatif, dan tanya.
  • Menguasai aturan penambahan -ing (termasuk kata-kata dengan perubahan ejaan).
  • Mengenali signal words khas Present Continuous (now, right now, at the moment, dst.).
  • Menguasai 20 kosakata tentang aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung.

📖 Theory

Present Continuous dipakai untuk sesuatu yang sedang berlangsung — berbeda dengan Present Simple yang membicarakan kebiasaan. Ada empat fungsi utama:

1 · Actions now

Hal yang sedang terjadi persis saat ini.

I am reading a book right now.

Saya sedang membaca buku sekarang.

2 · Temporary

Situasi sementara, belum tentu terjadi persis detik ini.

She is living in London for a few months.

Dia sedang tinggal di London untuk beberapa bulan.

3 · Changing

Sesuatu yang sedang berubah secara bertahap.

The weather is getting colder.

Cuacanya semakin dingin.

4 · Near future

Rencana yang sudah pasti/diatur untuk waktu dekat.

We are meeting them tomorrow.

Kita akan bertemu mereka besok.

Rumus (Formula)

Positive

Struktur S + am/is/are + V-ing
I am + V-ing
he, she, it is + V-ing
you, we, they are + V-ing

I am studying English now.

Saya sedang belajar bahasa Inggris sekarang.

She is cooking dinner.

Dia sedang memasak makan malam.

They are watching TV.

Mereka sedang menonton TV.

Negative

Struktur S + am/is/are + not + V-ing
I am not + V-ing
he, she, it isn't (is not) + V-ing
you, we, they aren't (are not) + V-ing

I am not sleeping.

Saya sedang tidak tidur.

He isn't playing football.

Dia sedang tidak bermain sepak bola.

Question

Struktur Am/Is/Are + S + V-ing ?
I Am I + V-ing?
he, she, it Is he/she/it + V-ing?
you, we, they Are you/we/they + V-ing?

Are you listening to me?

Apakah kamu sedang mendengarkan aku?

Is she working right now?

Apakah dia sedang bekerja sekarang?

Aturan Penambahan -ing

Pola Contoh
Kata kerja biasa + ing work → working, read → reading
Berakhiran -e (huruf e mati) → hilangkan e, + ing make → making, write → writing
1 suku kata, pola konsonan–vokal–konsonan → double konsonan terakhir + ing run → running, swim → swimming
Berakhiran -ie → ubah jadi y + ing lie → lying, die → dying

Signal Words

now · right now · at the moment · currently · today · Look! · Listen!

Posisi: biasanya di awal atau akhir kalimat.

Right now, I am doing my homework.

posisi di awal kalimat

She is sleeping at the moment.

posisi di akhir kalimat

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Kesalahan yang sering dilakukan penutur bahasa Indonesia saat belajar Present Continuous:

  • She cooking → She is cooking
    Lupa memakai to be (am/is/are) sebelum V-ing.
  • I am study → I am studying
    Lupa menambahkan -ing pada kata kerja.
  • He is plays football → He is playing football
    Setelah to be, kata kerja pakai -ing, bukan +s.
  • I am not knowing the answer → I don't know the answer
    Stative verbs (know, like, want, believe) biasanya TIDAK dipakai dalam bentuk continuous.
  • She is writeing a letter → She is writing a letter
    Huruf e dihilangkan sebelum + ing.

🇬🇧 British English Notes

Struktur grammar Present Continuous sama persis di British dan American English — perbedaannya di kosakata yang biasa dipakai dalam contoh:

  • flat (British) vs apartment (American) → "She is cleaning her flat."
  • holiday (British) vs vacation (American) → "We are planning a holiday."
  • queue (British) vs line (American) → "They are queuing for tickets."
  • trainers (British) vs sneakers (American) → "He is wearing his trainers."
  • Ejaan British dipertahankan untuk kata dengan -s- di tempat -z- Amerika, mis. practising (bukan "practicing").

✍️ Exercises

Kerjakan dulu tanpa melihat Answer Key. Klik "Periksa jawaban" untuk cek isian singkat.

Easy Easy

Lengkapi dengan am/is/are + V-ing yang tepat.

  1. I (study) for my exam right now.
  2. They (watch) a movie at the moment.
  3. My mother (cook) dinner in the kitchen.
  4. The children (play) in the garden.
  5. He (drive) to work today.

Medium Medium

Bentuk kalimat negatif dan tanya.

  1. She (not / sleep) — she is reading a book.
  2. you (listen) to music right now?
  3. We (not / talk) about that now.
  4. he (write) an email at the moment?
  5. I (not / eat) — I'm just drinking water.

Hard Hard

Perbaiki kesalahan atau susun kalimat, perhatikan aturan ejaan -ing.

  1. Perbaiki: "She is runing to catch the bus."
  2. Perbaiki: "They is chatting in the corner."
  3. Susun kalimat: now / a letter / writing / is / she.
  4. Ubah menjadi kalimat tanya: "He is practising the guitar."
  5. Tulis 1 kalimat untuk masing-masing fungsi: actions happening now, temporary situation, changing situation, near future arrangement. Jawaban bebas — akan disimpan otomatis di perangkatmu.

🏠 Homework

Lihat sekelilingmu di rumah dan tulis 10 kalimat tentang apa yang sedang dilakukan orang-orang di sekitarmu sekarang. Contoh: "My mother is cooking dinner." · "My brother is playing games."

Tersimpan otomatis di perangkatmu.
